Challenge Introduction

Sharif Artificial Intelligence Challenge 2017

Scientific competitions have always played a significant role in enhancing the university experience. It was the lack of such competitions for computer majors that prompted the Student's Scientific Chapter(SSC) of the Department of Computer Engineering of Sharif University of Technology, to hold competitions similar to the "Java Challenge". The 9th Java Challenge will be held in February and March 2017 and will be supporting C++ and Python for the 3rd consecutive year. The ''Java Challenge'', which is being held internationally for the second time, is a student programming contest in which teams of three, develop a program in one of Java, C++ or Python languages to overcome rivals one after another in a pre-designed battlefield. Since minimum programming skills in only one of the languages mentioned above is sufficient for entering this competition, "Sharif AI Challenge" is a good opportunity for all Computer major students to put their skills and abilities to test . You can see participation rules here . Hope to see you soon.

History

A brief look on the previous years

  • 2009 - 2011

    First Three Years

    In the University

    8 years ago, the need for an exciting and challenging inter-university competition in the CE department was a starting point for the "Sharif AI Challenge". This later became possible thanks to the efforts of the Student's Scientific Chapter(SSC) of this department. The students took advantage of this opportunity in order to evaluate their skills in programming and improve upon them. Participation was so impressive that the "Sharif AI Challenge" soon became an annual event.

  • 2012 - 2014

    4th to 6th Challenge

    With Teams From Others Universities of Tehran

    The 4th year of the competition was introduced as the "Java Challenge". Due to its high level and credibility, the event went further than collegiate level and witnessed the participation of other teams from Tehran. The 5th tournament was organized and held in a similar way. During all this time, the level and quality of the "AI Challenge" was immensely improved. With the efforts of the Student's Scientific Chapter of the CE department and widespread advertisement, the 6th year was finally organized and held nationally. The participation of teams from Tehran and other provinces brought upon a new spirit of nationality to the competition.

  • 2015

    7th year

    Supporting C++ and Python

    "Sharif AI Challenge" national competition continuously advanced and in its 7th year , thanks to the efforts of the technical team, C++ and Python were added to the competition's supported languages as well. This round of the "Sharif AI Challenge" was met with good reception and approximately 158 teams from 28 universities participated in the selection phase.
    The Documentation of this competition (Java Challenge 2015) is available here.

  • 2016

    8th year

    Aim to be International

    Since the added benefit of supporting other languages in the competition was a success, the 8th tournament was held under the name "Sharif AI Challenge". Furthermore, with the great advances made in the previous turn, the competition expanded internationally.
